How rankings work

  • Price - The price of the course. Pricing ranges from $ to $$$$$.
  • Handicap Difficulty - The general difficulty of the course. 1-18 with 18 being the easiest.
  • Hazard Difficulty - How many sleeves of balls you will need to get through this one. 1-5.

The rest of the rankings follow the S -> A -> B -> C -> D -> F tiering scale. With S being the best and F being the worst.
